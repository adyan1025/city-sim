About

Try your hand at creating your own city! Unlock props with the revenue you get to make your city look fabulous! City Simulation is an idle game where you accumulate money in order to unlock buildings and vehicles to add to your city.

Rules

You are given a blank slate. Generate money via the green button. In the menu, you have different props for you to buy. You can only buy the next one after you have bought the previous prop. Props consist of buildings and vehicles. You can review the Feed in the menu in order to track what each purchased prop does. Anytime you buy a prop it will be added to the visuals.

Buildings

Buildings grant you passive income. Without having to click the button manually, you will be able to earn a set amount of money per second.

Vehicles

Vehicles adds more money per click. For instance, every time you click after buying the Balloon vehicle, you will gain $2 per every click. Every new vehicle you buy increases with the previous vehicle as well.
